URL Shortener Microservice

URL Request

To request a new mini URL, add /route?url=<a valid url> to the address bar and press return. For example:
https://arwl-minurl.herokuapp.com/route?url=https://www.regextester.com/20
Open in another tab

Consuming the service

Your mini URL will automatically redirect you to the original webpage.
